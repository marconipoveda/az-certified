- [ ] Azure Hybrid Benefit
- [x] Azure Reservations
- [ ] Spot VMs
- [ ] Free Tier

**Explanation:**
Reservations lock in discounted rates for committed usage; Hybrid Benefit reuses licenses, Spot VMs are unused capacity without commitment.
